Algoritma \ref{alg:AlgoritmaGMM} di bawah ini adalah algoritma GMM.

\begin{minipage}{\textwidth}
  \begin{algorithm}[H]
    \captionof{algorithm}{Algoritma GMM}
    \label{alg:AlgoritmaGMM}
    \begin{algorithmic}
      \State {$x = 2$}
      \For {$i = 1 : 10$}
        \If {$x > 6$}
          \State {$x = x-2$}
        \Else
          \State {$x = x^2$}
        \EndIf
      \EndFor
    \end{algorithmic}
  \end{algorithm}
